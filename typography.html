reflection

Working on these typography pieces was very uniquely challenging for me, and, for some pieces, I went through multiple iterations and variations. Due to the variety of possible colour and layout combinations, even following discussions from my tutors, it was hard to determine what truly works best.

I am, however, proud of my final compositions and believe they fit the different typographic systems well. Due to the theme of the event, I really experimented with the idea of my pieces resembling a spool of thread, which has resulted in pleasing, unique artworks.
